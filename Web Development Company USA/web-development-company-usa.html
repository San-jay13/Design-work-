Is Your Current Website Quietly Costing You

While running a website, you must be cautious. You may be investing in marketing, SEO, and paid ads, but if your website isn't performing, then there's no profit to be gained. These are the warning signs you watch for before consulting a web development company in USA.

01

Load Time Over 3 Seconds

53% of mobile visitors leave before the page finishes loading, meaning every extra second of load time is a revenue leak.

02

Broken Mobile Experience

Most visitors browse on mobile. So, if your website isn't built for smaller screens, you're losing an opportunity on every visit.

03

Google Can't Read Properly

Bloated JavaScript, missing structured data, and duplicate meta tags are clearly visible to Google, and not in a good way.

04

Missing Conversion Path

Even though you managed to get targeted customers to land on your page, confused users can't convert. They'll leave anytime.

05

Security Vulnerabilities

Old WordPress plugins, outdated SSL, and unpatched themes are an open invitation and loses Google rankings overnight.

06

No Analytics Worth Reading

If your Google Analytics shows nothing useful over these months, then you're not making SEO decisions wisely.

If you face more than one issue that's been listed here, then you need to consider an expert web development company in USA, either nearshore or offshore, ASAP.
