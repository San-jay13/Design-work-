How Our Egg Traceability Software Supports FSMA 204 Compliance

Eggs are included on the FDA Food Traceability List (FTL) and businesses should maintain lot-level traceability records across key supply chain events. Our egg traceability software helps producers, packers, & distributors capture, organize, and retrieve the records needed to support FSMA 204 compliance.

Key Traceability Requirements

Critical Tracking Event (CTE) Key Data Elements (KDEs)
Farm / Harvesting Flock ID, collection date, farm location
Cooling & Storage Temperature records, facility details
Grading & Packing Lot code, packing date, grade classification
Shipping & Receiving Origin, destination, quantity, lot reference

How We Help

  • ✓ Maintain traceability lot records across every stage
  • ✓ Capture KDEs throughout the supply chain
  • ✓ Access FDA-ready traceability records on demand
  • ✓ Identify missing or incomplete traceability data
  • ✓ Retrieve lot history, shipment, and audit records from one platform
