What Kills DIY Ecommerce Platform Migration?

At Sparkout, we cleaned up “after failed migrations” and acted as an ecommerce project recovery service more times than we’d like to count. Here’s what actually goes out of hand when teams handle the migration by themselves or with the wrong hire.

1. Redirect

(-65% Organic Traffic)

Even a single missed 301 redirection can tank your SEO and vanish years of Google rankings overnight.

2. Live Store

(Data Corruption)

Migrating during an active sales period totally corrupts the order data and will create a digital nightmare.

3. Passwords

(-43% Repeat Orders)

Forcing thousands of active customers to reset their passwords kills the repeat buys and brand loyalty.

4. Launch Timing

($240 Lost Revenue)

Launching on a random Friday without monitoring increases the risk that a broken checkout will go unnoticed.

5. Images

(-38% Conversion Rate)

Non-optimized product images can literally affect your page's speed and lead to kill your conversion rates.

6. Order History

(Support Crisis)

Completely ditching your order history leaves the customer support completely blind when the day arrives.

7. Load testing

(Total Downtime)

Skipping performance testing will lead to your migrated website crashing under normal launch traffic.

8. Tax Logic

(Legal Exposture)

Ignoring platform-specific tax rules will possibly result in incorrect tax collection and a serious audit in the future.
